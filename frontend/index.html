Warranty & Services

1-Year Limited Warranty Protection

Enjoy Peace of Mind

Every vehicle purchased directly through our website is backed by a comprehensive 1-Year Limited Warranty starting from the original purchase date. We stand behind our machines to keep your adventures going.

Frame & Fork Coverage

Guaranteed structural integrity against design defects or material failures.

Mechanical Components Coverage

Covers drivetrain, transmission components, and structural mechanical parts.

Manufacturing Defect Protection

Coverage against errors in workmanship and initial assembly defects.

This warranty applies only to the original purchaser and is non-transferable.

Not Covered

  • Tires, Tubes, Brake Pads & Chains
  • Accidental Damage
  • Improper Assembly
  • Abuse or Neglect
  • Unauthorized Modifications
  • Stunt Riding Damage
